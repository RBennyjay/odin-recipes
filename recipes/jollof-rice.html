Jollof Rice

Jollof (/dʒəˈlɒf/), or jollof rice, is a rice dish from West Africa. The dish is typically made with long-grain rice, tomatoes, chilies, onions, spices, and sometimes other vegetables and/or meat in a single pot, although its ingredients and preparation methods vary across different regions.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Tomatoes paste
  • Onions
  • Red bell pepper
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • Salt and spices
  • Greyfish and fish
  • Groundnut oil

Steps

  1. Add 2 cups of parbioled rice to pot
  2. Add water and boil for about 5 minutes
  3. Wash it clean, add water and ingredients in right proportion
  4. Cook and taste to confirm its soft and tasty
  5. Dish it out and enjoy
  6. This is Nigerian Jollof Rice
